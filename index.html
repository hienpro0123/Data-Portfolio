About Me

Hi, I’m Trinh Quang Hien, a third-year Data Science student at the Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh City with a strong interest in Data Engineering. I focus on building reliable end-to-end data pipelines and scalable ETL workflows using Python, SQL, Apache Airflow, and Docker.
